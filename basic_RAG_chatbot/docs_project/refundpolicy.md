# Refund Policy

Customers may request a refund within seven calendar days after payment if project work has not yet started.

Once development begins, refunds are calculated based on completed milestones.

Completed milestones are non-refundable.

If TechNova Solutions is unable to complete a project due to internal reasons, a partial or full refund may be issued depending on project progress.

Custom software, AI models, integrations, and consulting services are generally non-refundable once delivered.

Refund requests should be submitted via email to support@technova.com along with the project details.
