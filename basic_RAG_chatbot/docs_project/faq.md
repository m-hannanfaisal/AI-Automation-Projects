# Frequently Asked Questions

## How long does a website project take?

Most business websites require two to four weeks depending on complexity.

## Do you provide source code?

Yes. Clients receive complete source code after the final payment.

## Which technologies do you use?

We primarily use React, Next.js, Node.js, Python, FastAPI, PostgreSQL, Docker, and cloud services.

## Can you redesign an existing website?

Yes. Existing websites can be redesigned without affecting business operations.

## Do you provide hosting?

Yes. We offer managed cloud hosting services for clients.

## Is technical support included?

Three months of technical support are included with most development projects.

## Do you sign NDAs?

Yes. Non-disclosure agreements are available before project discussions.
