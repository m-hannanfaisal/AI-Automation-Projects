# Careers

TechNova Solutions regularly hires software engineers, UI/UX designers, QA engineers, DevOps engineers, AI engineers, and digital marketers.

Internship programs are offered throughout the year for university students interested in software development and artificial intelligence.

Applicants should have strong problem-solving skills, communication abilities, and willingness to learn new technologies.

Internships usually last three months with the possibility of full-time employment based on performance.

Interested candidates can send their resume and portfolio to careers@technova.com.
